Game Manual

Everything you need to know — rules, scoring, and special features

Objective

Be the first player to reach 70 points by moving your pieces across the board, capturing enemy pieces, and reaching the opponent's back row. The exact moment a player hits 70 points, the game ends immediately — instant win.

The Board & Pieces

The game is played on a 6x6 grid. Each player starts with 6 pieces:

▲
You (Human) — Blue Upward Triangles, starting at the bottom row
▼
AI (Computer) — Red Downward Triangles, starting at the top row

How to Move

  • Click on one of your blue pieces to select it — it will highlight in Gold
  • Valid move destinations will highlight in Green
  • You can move one square orthogonally (Up, Down, Left, Right) into an empty space
  • Capturing: Jump over an adjacent enemy piece if the space directly behind them is empty. The enemy piece is removed from the board
  • Click the same piece again to deselect, or click another blue piece to switch selection

Scoring System

  • +1 Point — For making a standard move to an empty adjacent square
  • +2 Points — For capturing an enemy piece by jumping over it
  • +5 Points — For reaching the opponent's absolute back row (Row 0 for Human, Row 5 for AI)

Special Features

  • 30-Second Timer: You have 30 seconds to make your move. If time runs out, your turn is automatically skipped. Timer turns red below 5 seconds
  • Undo Button: Click "Undo Move" to take back your last turn. This undoes both your move and the AI's response, and resets your timer. One undo per turn
  • Instant Win: The exact moment a player hits 70 points, the game ends immediately with a victory popup
  • New Game: Click "New Game" at any time to reset everything and start fresh

Win Conditions

  • Reach 70 points before the AI — instant victory
  • Eliminate all AI pieces from the board
  • Leave the AI with zero valid moves on their turn

If none of the above apply when the game ends naturally, the player with the higher score wins. Equal scores result in a tie.

Quick Reference
Core Mechanics

Master these six mechanics to dominate the board

Orthogonal Movement

Move one square in any cardinal direction — up, down, left, or right — into any adjacent empty space.

Jump Captures

Leap over an adjacent enemy piece if the space behind them is empty. The enemy is removed — +2 points.

Back Row Bonus

Get any piece to the opponent's absolute last row for a massive +5 point bonus.

Click to Play

Click a blue piece to select (gold). Valid moves glow green. Click destination to move.

Win at 70 Points

The moment any player reaches 70 points, the game ends instantly. Every point matters.

Elimination Wins

If all of one player's pieces are captured, or they have zero valid moves, the game ends.

Under the Hood
Minimax AI Engine

The AI uses the classic Minimax algorithm with depth 2 to evaluate all possible move sequences, choosing the path that maximizes its advantage.

Minimax Search (Depth 2)

Evaluates all possible two-move sequences — AI move + Human response

Positional Evaluation

Score = (AI score - Human score) + row advancement bonus for each piece

Deep Copy Simulation

Each branch uses copy.deepcopy to simulate moves without altering the real board

Pure Python + Tkinter

No external game libraries — just tkinter widgets and standard library modules
