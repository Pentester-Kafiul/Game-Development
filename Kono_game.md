GAME MANUAL / INSTRUCTIONS

Battle Kono AI - How to Play

Objective
Be the first player to reach 150 points by moving your pieces across the board, capturing enemy pieces, and reaching the opponent's back row.


The Board & Pieces

The game is played on a 6x6 grid.
You (Human): Blue Upward Triangles (▲) starting at the bottom row.
AI (Computer): Red Downward Triangles (▼) starting at the top row.

How to Move

Click on one of your blue pieces to select it (it will highlight in Gold).
Valid moves will highlight in Green.
You can move one square orthogonally (Up, Down, Left, Right) into an empty space.
Capturing: You can jump over an adjacent enemy piece if the space directly behind them is empty. The enemy piece is removed from the board.

Scoring System

+1 Point: For making a standard move.
+2 Points: For capturing an enemy piece.
+5 Points: For reaching the opponent's absolute back row (Row 0 for Human, Row 5 for AI).

Special Features

⏱ 30-Second Timer: You have 30 seconds to make your move. If time runs out, your turn is automatically skipped.
↩ Undo Button: Made a mistake? Click "Undo Move" to take back your last turn (this will undo both your move and the AI's response, resetting your timer).
🏆 Instant Win: The exact moment a player hits 150 points, the game ends immediately.
